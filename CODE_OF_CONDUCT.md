# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want VOIDcore to be a project where people can contribute, ask questions,
and disagree about design without being treated poorly.

Please:

- be respectful and specific
- criticize ideas, not people
- assume good faith, but stay honest
- keep discussions useful for the next person reading them

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or intimidation
- personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- deliberate disruption of discussion or collaboration
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, close threads, or remove
contributors from project spaces if behavior makes collaboration unsafe or
unworkable.
